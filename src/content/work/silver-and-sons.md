## Background

Silver and Sons is a barbecue restaurant in the DMV, led by Jarrad Silver.

The food was already worth talking about. The job was making sure the talking scaled with the business, and pointed at the brand rather than one person.

## The work

This is an ongoing brand and social engagement.

Brand strategy separated the restaurant's identity from the founder's personal feed. Creative strategy and organic social built the content engine. Influencer partnerships extended reach into audiences that already trust local food voices. The whole program was pointed at recognition, because recognition is what makes a second location viable and a third one obvious.
