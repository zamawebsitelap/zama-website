## Background

Founded in 1953 and located on Ivy Street SE, the National Democratic Club is a private membership institution serving Capitol Hill professionals through dining, events, and member programming.

## The work

This is an ongoing communications and event partnership. Email and newsletters drive weekly member engagement. Creative and brand strategy modernize voice and visibility. Event and sponsorship strategy scale the annual Congressional Baseball after party into a flagship membership moment.

## The After Party

The annual Congressional Baseball after party now draws over 1,500 guests each June and has expanded sponsorship depth, including The Macallan and a McLaren activation.
