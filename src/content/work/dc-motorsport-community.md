## Background

DC Motorsport Community is a grassroots collective founded by Niki Lang to celebrate motorsport culture across the DMV. The opportunity was to turn Monaco weekend into a signature destination event in Washington, DC that could grow every year.

## The work

We ran this as an integrated brand, partnership, and communications program. Identity, sponsorship strategy, PR, social, and event execution were planned as one system so each year compounded instead of resetting.

## Growth Timeline

- 2024: Crystal City Sports Pub, Arlington - 3-hour morning race screening
- 2025: Champagne brunch format - expanded experience
- 2026: Casamara Rooftop, Dupont Circle - full-day event with champagne brunch, caviar service, DJ day party, charity auction, and 300 plus VIP guests
